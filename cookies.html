Your Choices Regarding Cookies

Upon your first visit to our website, you will be prompted to accept or decline cookies. If you choose to accept, we will store cookies on your device to improve your experience. If you choose to decline, only essential cookies will be used to enable the core functionalities of the site.

If you would like to change your cookie preferences, you can do so by clearing your browser's cache and refreshing the website, which will prompt the cookie consent message again.
